Round one of the BMW International Open got
  underway this morning. The first group of the day included German
  Hurly Long. The 26-year-old from Heidelberg made a great start to the
  iconic tournament at Golfclub München Eichenried. He carded an opening
  round of 68 (-4) to head to the clubhouse tied in eighth place. New
  Zealand’s Ryan Fox (-6) is the man currently at the top of the
  leaderboard. Billy Horschel (USA) finished his very first tournament
  round at the BMW International Open on -2 (70 shots). Below, you will
  find the latest images from the morning’s groups.
